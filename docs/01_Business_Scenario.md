# 1. Business Scenario

A motorcycle spare parts company operates with a large inventory and multiple suppliers. The company wants to improve its inventory replenishment process by digitalizing manual operations.

## Business Scenario

- The company manages hundreds of motorcycle spare parts across different product categories.
- Operational data is maintained in separate datasets, including products, suppliers, inventory, sales transactions, and purchase transactions.
- Inventory planners manually review stock levels and sales history to determine reorder quantities.
- The manual process is time-consuming, prone to inconsistent decisions, and becomes difficult to manage as transaction volumes increase.
- The company plans to implement a Microsoft Power Platform solution to automate inventory replenishment, improve data visibility, and support better purchasing decisions.
